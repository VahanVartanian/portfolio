why graphic and web design?

Web design and graphic design go together like coffee and mornings. It’s not just about making things look good but how everything works together as a whole. Since I’ve worked in both web development and graphic design, I’m able to take creative ideas and make them practical and functional.

I’m hoping to connect with clients and land an internship with a company to put these skills to work. Whether it’s building a clean, functional website, creating graphics, or making sure everything runs nice and smooth, I want to help businesses stand out online. Combining creativity and functionality to create something that really works is what I am passionate about.

I’ve always been drawn to computers and digital media from a young age. My curiosity for creating things on the screen led me to teach myself Photoshop when I was just 10 years old. I spent countless hours experimenting with digital tools, learning how to turn my young ideas into visuals. That early passion has only grown stronger over the years, shaping my skills in both graphic design and web development, and pushing me to constantly improve and expand my creative abilities.
