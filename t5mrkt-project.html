The goal of this project was to improve T5MRKT’s Shopify site to make shopping smoother and more appealing. This included creating custom collapsible product drawers, updating the About and Shipping pages, and boosting SEO for better visibility. New graphics and a simpler design also helped strengthen T5MRKT’s brand identity, giving customers a clearer, more enjoyable experience.

process.

I started by reviewing the existing Shopify theme and backing it up. This gave me a safe environment to experiment in without affecting the live website. I gathered feedback from stakeholders, noting priority items like the homepage layout, navigation flow, and brand presentation. This phase gave everyone clear goals and ensured we stayed aligned as I moved forward.

Next, I redesigned the homepage to highlight different engaging call to actions with refreshed banners, and placed brand call-to-action buttons in more visible positions on the brand tiles. Regular check-ins with stakeholders helped me refine the new homepage design and make sure it fit with T5MRKT’s look and feel.

To simplify product information, I introduced collapsible drawers for descriptions, making it easier for visitors to view details without scrolling endlessly. Icons and bullet points were also added to highlight key product features. This helped customers find what they needed quickly, improving the overall shopping experience. Since T5MRKT's catalogue includes a variety of products, I dynamically sourced each product's description from the Shopify backend meta fields, so the information will always be up to date, relevant, and efficient.

Because these pages are crucial for building trust, I revamped the about and shipping info page, and added engaging visuals, clear brand listings, and easy-to-read shipping policies. The goal was to make sure customers quickly find the information they need and feel confident in T5MRKT’s overall brand.
