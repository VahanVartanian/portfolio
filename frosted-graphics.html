Frosted Skateboards is a skateboarding and clothing brand based in Montreal, that is known for their edgy aesthetic. The goal of this project was to create a graphic to put on clothing for the brand, while also showcasing my expertise in graphic design and visual arts. Using tools like Procreate, Photoshop, and Illustrator, I created a unique design that reflects the rebellious spirit of skateboarding culture, and Frosted's brand identity.

process.

I started by sketching out different design ideas in Procreate that would fit with Frosted's aesthetic. To make sure the typography represented the sharpness and energy associated with skateboarding, I experimented with various fonts and styles, and created a solid foundation for this letter based design.

After narrowing down the sketch to a single concept, I adjusted and refined it even further by experimenting with proportions, angles, and spacing. This part involved making adjustments so that the letters flowed together while keeping it consistent.

Once the design felt polished, I transferred it to Illustrator to create a scalable vector version. This also let me fine-tune the curves and edges, so the graphic was clean and ready for use on clothing.
